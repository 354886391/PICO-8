## Super / Reverse Super

> ![超级跳](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/celestegame/images/a/a0/Backwards_Super.gif/revision/latest?cb=20190209062728)
>
> 1. 向左或向右冲刺
> 2. 玛德琳必须在冲刺结束前接触地面。
> 3. 在冲刺结束前起跳
>
> A super is performed by dashing on the ground followed by a jump, which results in a long-distanced jump.(super 是通过在地面上冲刺然后跳跃来执行的，这会导致远距离跳跃。)
>
> A reverse super is a super in the opposite direction, which can be done by switching directions when jumping (ex. dash right, then jump left). A super travels the same distance as simply jumping and then dashing.(reverse super 是相反方向的 super，可以通过跳跃时切换方向来完成（例如向右冲刺，然后向左跳）。supers 的距离与简单跳跃然后冲刺的距离相同。)

## Extended Super / Reverse Extended Super

> ![Extended Super](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/celestegame/images/3/3c/ExtendedReverseSuper.gif/revision/latest?cb=20181208040846)
>
> 1. 斜向下冲刺
> 2. 玛德琳必须在冲刺结束前接触地面。
> 3. 在冲刺结束前起跳
>
> After dashing, Madeline's hair turns white for a small window before it becomes red again. If you do a super and jump while Madeline's hair is white, you can do a super and retain your dash, which is called an extended super. The ability to retain a dash after a super can allow the player to cross larger gaps than a normal super could.(冲刺后，玛德琳的头发有一个白的时间窗口，然后再变红。 如果你在玛德琳的头发变白时做一个 super 并跳跃，你可以做一个 super 并保留你的冲刺，这被称为 extended super。 在超级之后保留 dash 的能力可以让玩家跨越比 normal super 更大的差距。)
>
> The player can also reverse an extended super to do a reverse extended super, which can allow the player to re-position themselves or do an extended super off of a wall next to the ground.(反向 extended super 可以让玩家重新定位自己或在靠近地面的墙上做extended super。)

## Hyper / Reverse Hyper

> ![Extended Super](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/celestegame/images/2/27/Hyper.gif/revision/latest?cb=20181208041904)
>
> A hyper is performed by dashing down diagonally and then jumping before the dash ends. Hypers function much like supers, but give the player an increase in speed that is significantly greater than either a super or dash. Madeline travels lower when performing a hyper, allowing her to jump through tighter gaps than a super. However, this lower jump height may cause the player to hit obstacles that are low to the ground if they are not careful.(通过沿对角线向下冲刺然后在冲刺结束之前跳跃来执行 hyper。 Hypers 的功能与 supers 非常相似，但给玩家的速度提升明显大于 super 或 dash。 玛德琳在执行 hyper 时会走得更低，这让她可以跳过比 super 更窄的缝隙。 但是，如果玩家不小心，这种较低的跳跃高度可能会导致玩家撞到离地面低的障碍物。 )
>
> As with supers, it is possible to do a reverse hyper by switching directions when jumping, although this has limited practical value.(与 supers 一样，可以通过在跳跃时切换方向来进行 reverse hyper，尽管这具有有限的实用价值。)

## Wavedash / Reverse Wavedash

> ![Extended Super](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/celestegame/images/4/48/Wave_hyper.gif/revision/latest?cb=20190219130310)
>
> A wavedash can be performed by dashing down diagonally after a jump and then jumping almost instantly after landing. While a wavedash is similar in function to an extended hyper, it can be used in different scenarios. Unlike an extended hyper, it is possible to perform a wavedash on one single tile, while an extended hyper requires either more space or an adjacent wall to be performed. Wavedashes can also be reversed, much like extended hypers. (可以通过在跳跃后沿对角线向下冲刺然后在着陆后几乎立即跳跃来执行波浪冲刺。 虽然 wavedash 在功能上类似于扩展的 hyper，但它可以用于不同的场景。 与扩展的 hyper 不同，可以在单个瓷砖上执行 wavedash，而扩展的 hyper 需要更多空间或相邻的墙来执行。 Wavedashes 也可以反转，就像 extended hypers。)
>
> The relatively high technical difficulty of the wavedash may result in two common issues: the wavedash not performing, and the failure to retain the dash afterwards. The former is a result of the player initiating the downward diagonal dash too high off the ground. In this case, the dash has already finished once the player has touched the ground, thus resulting in a failure to perform the wavedash. The second problem, in which the wavedash is performed but without the retaining of the dash, is a result of the player initiating the downward diagonal dash too low off the ground. By jumping before Madeline's hair turns white, the dash is unable to be reset.(wavedash 相对较高的技术难度可能导致两个常见问题：wavedash 不执行，以及之后无法保留 dash。 前者是玩家开始向下对角线冲刺时离地太高的结果。 在这种情况下，一旦玩家接触地面，冲刺就已经完成，因此无法执行 wavedash。 第二个问题是执行了 wavedash 但没有保留 dash，这是因为玩家开始向下对角线 dash 离地面太低。 通过在玛德琳的头发变白之前跳跃，无法重置 dash。)

## Wallbounce

> ![Extended Super](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/celestegame/images/6/6e/Wallbounce.gif/revision/latest?cb=20190112102045)
>
> 1. 向上冲向墙边
> 2. 在冲刺结束前（冲刺后 14 帧内），玛德琳必须接触墙壁。
> 3. 在墙上冲刺结束前跳跃
>
> A wallbounce occurs when the player dashes upwards when adjacent to a wall and then jumps during the dash. The jump can be done at any time up until the end of the dash, causing the wallbounce to travel higher the longer the jump is delayed. Wallbounces allow the player to travel vertically more quickly than a dash or wallkick, and can be used in multiple levels to complete them faster.(当玩家在靠近墙壁时向上冲刺然后在冲刺过程中跳跃时会发生Wallbounce。 跳跃可以在冲刺结束前的任何时间进行，导致跳跃延迟的时间越长，wallbounce跳得越高。 Wallbounces 允许玩家比 dash 或 wallkick 更快地垂直移动，并且可以在多个关卡中使用以更快地完成它们。)

## Ultra

> ![Extended Super](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/celestegame/images/a/ae/Ultradash.gif/revision/latest?cb=20190112095941)
>
> An ultra is an oblique dash that occurs when performing a diagonal-down dash when already moving with a high horizontal velocity. This causes the player to maintain their speed and get a speed boost upon hitting the ground. Using a diagonal-up dash instead results in an "upwards ultra".(ultra 是在已经以快速水平移动时执行对角线向下冲刺时发生的斜向冲刺。 这会导致玩家保持速度并在撞到地面时获得速度提升。 相反，使用对角线向上的 dash 会导致“向上 ultra”。)

## Demodash

> ![Extended Super](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/celestegame/images/f/f0/Demodash.gif/revision/latest?cb=20190112100520)
>
>A demodash is performed by dashing down, then immediately (within 4 frames or 0.066 seconds, 0.068 seconds of in-game time) releasing the down key. This causes Madeline to dash horizontally while keeping a crouched state at the same time. Being crouched reduces the size of the player's hitbox, allowing them to fit through gaps that could not be travelled through under normal circumstances. If one not only releases the down key, but also inputs other directions, a demodash can be done upwards or diagonally up.(demodash 通过向下冲刺执行，然后立即（在 4 帧或 0.066 秒内，游戏时间的 0.068 秒内）释放向下键。 这会导致 Madeline 水平冲刺，同时保持蹲伏状态。 蹲伏会减小玩家碰撞箱的大小，使他们能够穿过正常情况下无法穿过的缝隙。 如果不仅松开向下键，还输入其他方向，则可以向上或对角向上进行demodash。)
>
> Since v1.4.0.0, the keybind "Crouch Dash" simplifies this move. With this keybind, a demodash can be performed by holding the key of the desired direction and pressing Crouch Dash. (This is unavailable on console)(从 v1.4.0.0 开始，快捷键“Crouch Dash”简化了这一动作。 使用此键绑定，可以通过按住所需方向的键并按 Crouch Dash 来执行 demodash。 （这在控制台上不可用）)

## Demohyper

> Attempting to perform a super out of a demodash will result in a hyper instead because the game checks for whether Madeline is crouched to determine which to perform. This hyper is referred to as a demohyper. Demohypers are slightly faster than ordinary hypers, because the player has greater horizontal velocity before it begins due to being in a horizontal dash rather than a diagonal one.(尝试在 demodash 中执行 super 将导致 hyper，因为游戏会检查 Madeline 是否蹲下以确定执行哪个。 这种 hyper 被称为demohyper。 Demohypers 比普通的 hypers 稍微快一点，因为玩家在开始之前由于处于水平冲刺而不是对角线冲刺而具有更大的水平速度。)

## Coyote Time

> Celeste has a "coyote time" mechanic where the player can jump in the air for 5 frames after falling off a platform. This can be used to obtain the maximum possible distance from an extended hyper. Coyote time also affects the jump used to trigger a hyper, allowing one to start the hyper from midair from a short distance beyond a platform.(Celeste 有一个“土狼时间”机制，玩家可以在从平台上掉下来后在空中跳跃 5 帧。 这可用于获得与 extended hyper 相同的最大可能距离。 Coyote time 也会影响用于触发 hyper 的跳跃，允许人们从平台外的短距离从半空中启动 hyper。)

## Cornerboost

> ![Extended Super](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/celestegame/images/2/2f/Y_E_E_T.gif/revision/latest?cb=20190219125301)
>
> A cornerboost can be performed if the player climb-jumps off the top 5-7 pixels of a wall (usually 7 if the player is moving slowly, 5 if the player is moving quickly) with the right timing, this allows the player to maintain any previous horizontal momentum after the climb-jump.(如果玩家在正确的时间从墙的顶部 5-7 像素（如果玩家移动缓慢，通常为 7，如果玩家快速移动，通常为 5），则可以执行 cornerboost，这允许玩家保持 climb-jump 后任何先前的水平动量。)

## Neutral Jump

> ![Extended Super](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/celestegame/images/f/fa/Neutral.gif/revision/latest?cb=20181208042912)
>
> A neutral jump occurs when the player jumps away from a wall without inputting a direction or holding down the grab button, which causes the player to jump away from the wall by a smaller distance than a wallkick. This can allow the player to move back to the wall at the peak of the neutral jump, resulting in a higher vertical position. Unlike a climb-jump however, neutral jumping does not use up stamina, but nevertheless allows the player to travel vertically up the wall. Neutral jumps can also allow the player to climb walls when out of stamina or if the wall cannot be traversed with climb-jumps.(当玩家在没有输入方向或按住抓取按钮的情况下从墙上跳开时，就会发生 neutral jump，这会导致玩家从墙上跳开的距离比墙踢更小。 这可以让玩家在 neutral jump 的最高点移回墙壁，从而获得更高的垂直位置。 然而，与爬跳不同的是，neutral jump 不会消耗耐力，但仍然允许玩家垂直向上攀爬墙壁。 neutral jump 还可以让玩家在体力耗尽或无法通过攀爬跳跃穿越墙壁时攀爬墙壁。)

## Spikejump

> ![Extended Super](https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/celestegame/images/5/58/Wall_spikejump-0.gif/revision/latest?cb=20190112101550)
>
> A spikejump is a wallkick or neutral jump performed on a corner covered with spikes. This is possible because spikes do not harm Madeline when she is moving in the same direction they are pointing. As long as Madeline is moving in the same direction as the spikes, she can wall kick off of the wall behind the spikes. Spikejumps are often used in speedruns.(spikejump 是在布满尖刺的角落进行的 wallkick 或 neutral jump。 这是可能的，因为当玛德琳朝尖刺指向的相同方向移动时，尖刺不会伤害她。 只要玛德琳的移动方向与尖刺相同，她就可以从尖刺后面的墙上踢开。 Spikejumps 通常用于速通。)

## Ceiling Spikejump

> Spikejumps are also possible off of downward-facing corners. This is somewhat easier than a standard spikejump because it exploits the "ceiling pop" mechanic in which, when hitting the edge of a wall while moving upwards, the game will move Madeline left or right by up to 5 pixels in an attempt to avoid the wall.(Spikejumps 也可以从朝下的角落进行。 这比标准的 spikejump 更容易一些，因为它利用了“天花板弹出”机制，在该机制中，当向上移动时撞到墙的边缘时，游戏会将 Madeline 向左或向右移动最多 5 个像素，以避免 墙。)

## Screen Transitions

> Although not a move of its own, the exploiting of screen transitions is taught to the player by the game indirectly when figuring out how to get Chapter 2's Crystal Heart. When moving between screens, the game will reset the player's dash, allowing for unintended uses in many places to skip parts of a room.(虽然这不是它自己的举动，但在弄清楚如何获得第 2 章的水晶之心时，游戏会间接向玩家传授屏幕转换的利用。 在屏幕之间移动时，游戏将重置玩家的仪表板，允许在许多地方意外使用以跳过房间的某些部分。)

## 帧校正技术

> Buffer：在任何动作可能之前预压最多 4 帧。
Celeste 不像其他游戏那样接受连续跳跃或冲刺输入。必须释放并按下一次才能使用它。但是，通过预先输入，可以连续进行操作，没有一点时间差。这项技术是对游戏控制的修正，因此即使您提前最多 4 帧按下跳跃或冲刺，跳跃或冲刺也会被激活。另一方面，如果将实际激活操作的一帧加在一起，结果帧完美操作的范围是4+1=5帧。

> 缓冲跳跃：在玛德琳落地前最多跳跃 4 帧。
如果在着地前稍微按住跳跃键，一着地就可以进行帧完美跳跃，并且可以进行最佳兔子跳。您可以在不接触地面的情况下跳离地面 1 个像素，这是与上述空中超级破折号相关的修正。有没有着地，可以通过脚步声判断。同样的道理，你也可以进行缓冲跳墙，在撞墙前跳跃5帧，这样的话，即使你用梦方块或凯文冲刺，也可以不激活跳墙跳出去。

> 缓冲冲刺：最多冲刺 4 帧后才能冲刺。
如果梅德琳触地或吃掉冲刺恢复水晶，她可以再次冲刺。此时，如果她提前4帧按下冲刺，她可​​以在接触地面或吃掉水晶后立即冲刺。此外，由于一次破折号的持续时间为 14 帧，如果您想连续破折号，则第二个破折号必须执行第一个破折号并等到 15 帧。但是，例如，如果您在第 11 帧按下破折号，破折号会预先输入，第 15 帧一出现，完美帧的破折号就会准确出现。以此方式，可以通过预输入相对容易地实现帧完美或像素完美操作。

> Buffered Climb Jump：在 Madeline 攀爬时从墙上跳下，最多 5 帧后到达顶峰。
可以使用与跳跃优先输入或冲刺优先输入相同的原则提前输入攀登跳跃，但需要注意的是，如果玛德琳正在坠落，它就没有效果。你可以在不激活掉落方块、第 1 章交通灯方块、第 4 章自动移动方块、第 6 章冲刺方块和第 8 章熔岩方块的情况下通过先进入 Climb Jump 来攀爬。另外，即使在第 2 章直接冲向梦想方块，如果先进入攀爬跳跃，也可以不经过梦想方块而坚持下去。

> Buffered Hyper/Super：在冻结帧结束前冲刺然后跳跃。
这是一种立即使用 Hyper/Super 的技术。因为跳跃在破折号开始之前就结束了，如果你在梦想方块的顶部进行，你将不会进入梦想方块，如果你在第 5 章的交换方块之上使用它，你将不会收到块加速。

> Pause Buffering : 暂停释放动画结束前的预输入。
